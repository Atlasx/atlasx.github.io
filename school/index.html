School Projects Projects worked on as assignments at the University of Utah's EAE program

Parts Per Million

Created by a team of six students at the University of Utah's EAE program, Parts Per Million is an alternative game focused on demonstrating the effects of climate change. Players fight food shortages, explosive population growth, and natural disasters in an attempt to sustain their island.

Parts Per Million was made as the main assignment of Alternative Game Development FILM-3720 over the course of the Spring 2017 semester.

Veloces

Using the Unity3d game engine, Veloces was created as part of the University of Utah's EAE program. With two programmers and one artist, the game took on a smaller scope to have better chances of finishing a complete experience. Veloces is an isometric local multiplayer hack 'n slash designed to deliver exciting competitive co-op moments.

Veloces was made as the main assignment of Traditional Game Development FILM-3710 over the course of the Fall 2016 semester.
