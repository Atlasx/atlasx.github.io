Veloces

A fast paced couch co-op
with up to 4 players

With a team of two programmers and two artists at the University of Utah's EAE program, Veloces is a fast paced couch co-op game. Up to four players fight in a timed deathmatch gametype locally on PC or Mac. Players can use their stamina to shoot their gun, dash, slash, or stab at their enemies. Highest kills at the end of four minutes wins.

Controls Hack, slash, shoot, and stab



Slash
The basic attack for close-range combat



Stab
High damage attack



Shoot
Ranged attack which drains stamina
