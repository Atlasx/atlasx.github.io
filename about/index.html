About Me The man, the myth, the legend!

Connor J Douglas Programmer, Designer, Gamer, and more

Hey, I am Connor Douglas an aspiring game developer from Honolulu Hawaii going to the University of Utah for Computer Science. I found my love for computer science and game developing ever since I joined the Robotics team in highschool and have been going strong ever since.

With around 7 years of programming experience, I have a solid foundation upon which I can pursue a higher goal of creating compelling and fun video games. At the University of Utah, I have been able to join classes whose sole purpose is to create video games. In these classes I created the games Parts Per Million, a hex tile based game with a focus on environmental impacts.

Outside of classes I am an avid trombone player with over 11 years of practice and performances in London, New York, and Berklee.

I love to hear anything about game development, computer science, or any other interesting activities so send me a message on twitter or shoot me an email.
